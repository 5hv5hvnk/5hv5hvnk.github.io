Bio

Hi! I am a Pre-Doctoral Researcher at the Microsoft PROSE in Bengaluru, working on AI for Code, Automated Reasoning and Reliable Code Generation. I have been priveleged to work with Gustavo Soares, Arjun Radhakrishna, Sumit Gulwani and Arun Iyer.
Previously, I worked as a Research Engineer Intern at a non-profit startup, Wadhwani AI, where I focused on developing speech understanding solutions for Indic languages. Which led to standarized evaluations of elemantry student's language skills (more info)
During my undergraduate, I worked with Thomas Wiecki on PyMC which is a probabilistic programming package in python as a google summer of code contributor.
