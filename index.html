Bio

Hi! I am a Research Fellow (Pre-Doctoral) at Microsoft Research India. Previously, I was a Research Fellow with the program synthesis group (PROSE) at Microsoft in Bengaluru. I work on AI for Code and AI for Math. My research goal is to make automated programming more trustworthy by developing AI systems that generate robust, correct, and verifiable code—leveraging techniques from Formal Methods, Software Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence to ensure the reliability and correctness of automatically generated programs.

I have been privileged to work with Aditya Kanade, Ashish Tiwari, Gustavo Soares, Arjun Radhakrishna, Sumit Gulwani, and Arun Iyer.

I am applying for PhD. positions for fall 2026, in space of AI4Code, AI4Math and trustworthy ML. I will be at NeurIPS 2025, do reach out if you want to chat!!

For fun, I enjoy bouldering, running and playing chess.
